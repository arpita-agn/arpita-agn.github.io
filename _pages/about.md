I am a Software Engineer at **Dynamic Solution Innovators Ltd.** where I build enterprise web applications using Jakarta EE and JSF. My work spans full-stack development, dynamic report generation, and user acceptance testing for large-scale systems.

Beyond industry work, I am passionate about **quantum computing research**. My research focuses on empirical analysis of quantum kernel methods applied to gene expression data, with a publication at **IEEE WIECON-ECE 2025**. I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (CGPA: 3.80/4.00).

I am also an active competitive programmer with **500+ problems solved** across platforms like Vjudge, Codeforces, GeeksforGeeks, and LeetCode. I was selected as a scholar for the **Grace Hopper Celebration 2022**, the world's largest tech conference for women in computing.
